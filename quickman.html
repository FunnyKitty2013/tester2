QUICK MAN

Quick Man in Mega Man 2 is a high-speed Robot Master notoriously difficult to damage, but he is highly vulnerable to the Time Stopper weapon (Flash Man's weapon), which drains half his HP instantly. He is best fought using Time Stopper, Crash Bombs, or carefully timed shots from the Mega Buster.

Weaknesses and Tips

  • Best Weakness: Use Flash Man's Time Stopper to paralyze him. It drains ~50% of his life.
  • Secondary Weakness: High damage, but difficult to land on a fast target.
  • Third Weakness (Atomic Fire): A fully charged shot does significant damage.
  • Anti-Weakness Do not use Metal Blades; they bounce off him.
  • Strategy: Activate Time Stopper immediately, switch to Buster, and stand in a corner, waiting to jump over him

Stage Tips and Tricks

  • Freeze Timing: Start the Time Stopper just as he lands from a jump
  • Corner Strategy: Standing in the corner, jumping over him, and shooting in the back is effective.
  • Dodge Boomerangs: He throws 3 boomerangs, usually when at the peak of a jump.
  • The Stage: The stage features instant-death beams, making fast traversal necessary; consider saving a tiny bit of Time Stopper for the final beam section.
