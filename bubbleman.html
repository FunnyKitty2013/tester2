BUBBLE MAN

Bubble Man is one of the eight Robot Masters in Mega Man 2, designed by Dr. Wily for underwater combat. While fast in water, his inability to walk on land makes him a relatively easy boss if you know his patterns and weaknesses. His battle takes place in a submerged room with instant-death spikes on the ceiling.

Weaknesses and Tips

  • Primary Weakness (Metal Blade): The Metal Blade is devastating, killing him in 4 hits on Normal and 7 on Difficult. The best advantage is that you can shoot it diagonally upwards at him while he swims, avoiding the need to jump high into the spikes.
  • Secondary Weakness (Quick Boomerang): Similar to the Metal Blade, it pops his bubbles and deals high damage.
  • Tertiary Weakness (Crash Bomber): Shooting this at a wall where Bubble Man lands can deal multiple damage hits, but it is less reliable than the Metal Blade.
  • Do Not Use Bubble Lead: Using Bubble Lead against Bubble Man will heal him.

Stage Tips and Tricks

  • Weakness: Bubble Man is weak to the Bubble Blade (5 damage) and also vulnerable to the Crash Bomber.
  • The Cheese Strategy: Standing near the walls can bait him into jumping into the opposite wall and getting stuck.
  • Movement: He jumps over you if you get too close and changes the conveyor belt direction periodically.
  • Pattern: He throws two fast metal blades at a time.
