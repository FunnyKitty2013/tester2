HEAT MAN

Heat Man is a Zippo lighter-themed Robot Master in Mega Man 2 (DWN-019), designed by Dr. Wily for high-temperature combat. His stage is notorious for its challenging "disappearing block" puzzles and lava-filled pits, while he is best countered using the Bubble Lead weapon. Defeating him rewards the player with the Atomic Fire weapon and Item-1.

Stage Tips and Tricks

  • Conveyor Belts: Continuously jump to maintain control on the shifting conveyor belts.
  • Drill Hazards: Watch for consistent spawning patterns of drill bits from the ceiling/floor; move steadily to avoid them.
  • Gear Enemies: These gear-slowing enemies charge after being hit. Shoot them, then prepare to move.
  • Avoid Hidden Holes: Be aware of dangerous, hidden gaps in the floor.
  • Speedrunner Trick (Zip): Use Item-1 to glitch upward through the ceiling by aligning with background gears.

Heat Man Boss Fight

  • Weakness: Heat Man is weak to the Heat Blade (5 damage) and also vulnerable to the Crash Bomber.
  • The Cheese Strategy: Standing near the walls can bait him into jumping into the opposite wall and getting stuck.
  • Movement: He jumps over you if you get too close and changes the conveyor belt direction periodically.
  • Pattern: He throws two fast metal blades at a time.
